This variant is the compromise option: more recognizably “classic Cordier” than the current implementation, but still polished enough that it could plausibly ship with only moderate adaptation.

What Changed

The axis orientation and the descending optimum line match the older diagrams, while the filled efficiency field improves hierarchy on-screen.

Why It May Work

It preserves the recognizability of the reference chart without fully reverting to a monochrome scanned-paper look.

Tradeoff

The filled regions are a visual abstraction, so they still read as a modernized interpretation rather than a direct facsimile.

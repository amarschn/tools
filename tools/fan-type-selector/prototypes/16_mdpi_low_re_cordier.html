Prototype P: MDPI Low-Re Cordier Overlay

This prototype follows the logic of the MDPI paper more closely: start with a standard Balje map, then overlay shifted low-Re Cordier curves rather than pretending the whole chart is just one universal contour field.

Reference Energies 2015 Figure 8
Focus actual vs modified Cordier lines

The important visual change here is conceptual: the Balje map is the backdrop, and the Reynolds-dependent Cordier lines are overlays on top of it.

Why This Matches Better

The paper is not presenting a generic new atlas. It is comparing a standard map against a modified locus.

What Is Still Approximate

The specific low-Re shifts are reconstructed heuristically from the article’s description and figure geometry, not digitized from the authors’ original curves.

Prototype J: Cordier Type Bars

A stricter, more diagrammatic reading. Efficiency becomes subdued background structure, while the fan architectures are shown as specific-speed bars at the top of the chart.

Look monochrome technical plate
Focus family ranges first
Reference cue vertical type bars

This is the cleanest attempt if the production plot should feel more like a traditional selection chart and less like a modern app data-viz panel.

What Changed

The fan families move out of ribbons wrapped around the optimum line and into explicit Ns bars, which makes the classification logic easier to inspect.

Why It May Work

It separates two ideas cleanly: the Cordier optimum locus and the architecture-specific speed bands layered on top of it.

Tradeoff

It is less visually rich than the classic contour paper style, so it may feel more didactic than atmospheric.

Prototype B — Refit Coefficients (A=0.833, B=−0.594, C=0.019)

Quadratic refit to Balje's approximate reference data. Same correlation form, different B and C.

Original (current tool) ln(Ds) = 0.833 − 0.524 ln(Ns) + 0.008 [ln(Ns)]²
Refit (this prototype) ln(Ds) = 0.833 − 0.594 ln(Ns) + 0.019 [ln(Ns)]²
How the refit was derived: Three reference points were read from Balje (1981) Fig. 1.2: (Ns=0.1, Ds≈10), (Ns=1.0, Ds≈2.3), (Ns=10, Ds≈0.65). Solving the 3×3 system gives A=0.833, B=−0.594, C=0.019. The steeper B and larger C produce visible curvature — the slope in log-log goes from about −0.64 at Ns=0.1 to −0.51 at Ns=10.

Prototype F: Guided Narrative

Reads top-to-bottom like a decision memo: your input, what it means, how the types compare, what to check, and what to do next. Each step shows its conclusion on the fold line; expand only if you want the reasoning.

Pattern Timeline narrative with inline expand
Goal Tell the story of the decision, not just the answer
Takeaway

This reads like a short engineering memo. The user gets the conclusion at each step without expanding, but can open any step to see the supporting evidence — including equations with substituted values, weight rationale tables, and efficiency derivation. No tabs, no metric grid — just a linear decision story with arbitrary depth.

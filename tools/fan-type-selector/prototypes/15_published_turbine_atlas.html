Prototype O: Published Turbine Atlas

A direct attempt to match the older published chart language from the Concepts NREC turbine figure: white paper background, dense black linework, machine-class regions, and multiple contour families rather than one generic efficiency field.

Reference Concepts NREC turbine chart
Look scanned-book atlas

This one is intentionally not “clean app UI”. It tries to feel like a reproduced turbomachinery reference sheet, because that is what the source figure actually is.

Why This Is Better

The chart uses multiple distinct contour families and region labels, which is what made the published figure read correctly in the first place.

What Is Still Approximate

The curves are reconstructed from the visual geometry of the source image, not digitized from the original Balje dataset.

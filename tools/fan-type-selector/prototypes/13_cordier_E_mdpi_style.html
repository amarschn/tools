Prototype E — MDPI-style Filled Efficiency Islands

Inspired by the MDPI Energies 2015 right-panel Cordier diagram. Filled color-gradient efficiency islands with the Cordier line overlaid. Light engineering-chart background.

Key difference from Prototype D: Efficiency islands are filled with a color gradient (cool blue = low η, warm yellow/green = high η) similar to the colored contour maps in modern references. The Cordier line and type regions are drawn on top.

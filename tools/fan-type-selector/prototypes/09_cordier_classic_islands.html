This variant is the closest to the linked reference. The emphasis is on the traditional chart grammar, not on showing every interactive family ribbon from the production tool.

What Changed

The current production plot swaps the axes and leans on colored family territories. This version restores the classic descending map.

Why It May Work

It immediately reads as a Cordier diagram to anyone who has seen Balje-style turbomachinery charts before.

Tradeoff

The plot is less explicit about the tool’s heuristic family neighborhoods, so it behaves more like a technical atlas than a decision explainer.

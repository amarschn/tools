Prototype N: Cordier vs Balje, Same Axes

This page exists specifically to answer the confusion. Both charts use the same N_s-D_s axes. The left shows a Cordier-style reading; the right shows a Balje-style reading.

Left optimum path
Right efficiency field

The reason they look different is not that the axes changed. It is that the plot is carrying a different layer of information. Cordier is usually the best-efficiency locus and nearby family neighborhoods. Balje is the broader performance atlas wrapped around that locus.

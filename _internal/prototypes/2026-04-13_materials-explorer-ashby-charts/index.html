Materials Explorer / Prototype Studies

Ashby Chart Prototype Set

Three clean Plotly studies aimed at a more classic Ashby-chart feel. All three use the same materials dataset and the same stiffness map so the plotting language can be compared directly.

Attempt A

Smoothed Hull Bubbles

Family regions are derived from convex hulls in log space, expanded and smoothed into soft bubbles. Direct in-chart labels remove the need for a legend.

Open Attempt A
Attempt B

Covariance Ellipses

Each family is represented by a fitted log-space ellipse. The chart keeps a more formal legend and calls out exemplar materials.

Open Attempt B
Attempt C

Territory Ribbons

Family regions are built from smoothed upper and lower bounds across density, producing elongated map territories with a restrained monochrome presentation.

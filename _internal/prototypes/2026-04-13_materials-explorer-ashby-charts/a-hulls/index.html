What Changed

Family envelopes come from smoothed convex hulls in log space. This keeps the overall map organic and close to textbook Ashby charts without forcing a formal statistical fit.

Geometry Convex hull, expanded outward, then Chaikin-smoothed.
Labels Family names are embedded inside the bubbles.
Guides Dotted merit lines show the direction of higher specific stiffness.

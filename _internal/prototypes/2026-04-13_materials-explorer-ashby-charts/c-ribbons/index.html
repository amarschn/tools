What Changed

This version leans harder into clean territory maps. The family shapes are built from upper and lower bounds across density, which produces long horizontal ribbons closer to some printed Ashby atlases.

Geometry Density-binned upper/lower bounds merged into smooth ribbons.
Legend Moved out of the chart and replaced with a compact inline key.
Tone Lower-saturation fills, sharper outlines, and fewer labels.
